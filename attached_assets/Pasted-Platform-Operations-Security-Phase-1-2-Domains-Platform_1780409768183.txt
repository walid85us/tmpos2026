WORKING STYLE — THINK STEP BY STEP
==================================

Before answering, implementing, correcting, or fixing anything, think step by step.

Do not rush to code.

Before each milestone:

1. Inspect the current relevant files.
2. Identify current accepted behavior.
3. Identify what must remain locked.
4. Identify which data is real, mock, session-based, or derived.
5. Identify what is safe to implement in this milestone.
6. Implement only the current milestone.
7. Verify no accepted workstream regressed.
8. Stop and provide a milestone report.

Do not proceed to the next milestone without approval.

PRIMARY GOAL
============

Transform Domains and Platform Settings from simple admin surfaces into mature platform-governance workspaces.

The System Owner should understand:

Domains:

* Which tenant domains exist?
* Which domains are verified, pending, failed, disabled, or at risk?
* What DNS records are required?
* What SSL/TLS readiness state exists?
* What is manual vs future automated?
* What changed recently?
* Which tenant/domain needs action?
* Which domains are blocking tenant readiness or brand launch?
* What exact records should be copied?
* What is safe to do now vs future real provider integration?

Platform Settings:

* Which settings are documentation-only vs currently applied?
* Who changed a setting and when?
* What is the impact of a setting before saving?
* Which settings are risky or sensitive?
* Which settings are grouped by branding, security defaults, maintenance, support contacts, notifications, and future integrations?
* Which settings require audit visibility?
* Which settings are future placeholders and not enforced?
* What is the current platform configuration posture?

Use deterministic, explainable, session/app-state logic.
Do not add fake real-time provider checks.
