CURRENT QA SOURCE OF TRUTH — REMAINING FAILURES
==================================================

These are the exact remaining blockers and must be fixed literally:

1) Shipping Providers still also appears in Settings
- It now appears in Shipping Center, but it still remains in Settings
- The requirement is literal:
  Shipping Providers must be removed as a live management surface from Settings
- Shipping Center must be the only real home for provider management
- Settings must not still present provider management UI, cards, controls, or parallel access as a management destination

2) Label action is still not PDF-only
- QA still gets PNG
- Requirement is literal:
  the primary operational label action must open a real PDF artifact
- The label should open immediately for printing within the app flow using the PDF artifact
- The UI must not say PDF and open PNG
- The integration/output-selection must be corrected, not explained away

3) Destination address autocomplete still is not the required Google-style experience
- Current behavior is still the old OpenStreetMap/local suggestion approach
- Requirement is literal:
  replace the current autocomplete approach with Google-powered address autocomplete behavior
- When the user types destination address, a real Google-style address suggestion dropdown must appear
- Selecting one suggestion must prefill:
  - address line
  - city
  - state/region
  - postal code
  - other relevant address fields if available
- This must be a real visible autocomplete experience, not city/zip hinting only
- You may integrate Google Places / Google address autocomplete architecture or an equivalent Google-powered path that fits this app
- Do not fake suggestions

4) Ready button gating is still wrong
- QA now requires:
  Ready must be disabled until a shipping rate is selected
- Once the rate is selected:
  - Ready becomes enabled
  - then the shipment can proceed toward label purchase
- The workflow must be operationally coherent:
  packages → validated address → get rates → select rate → Ready enabled → purchase label
- Do not allow Ready prematurely before rate selection
