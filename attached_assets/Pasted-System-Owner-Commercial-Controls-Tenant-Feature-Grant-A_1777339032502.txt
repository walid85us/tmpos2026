INTENDED BUSINESS LOGIC
==================================================

For each tenant feature row:

1) Included by Plan
Display:
- Included by Plan
Actions:
- no Trial / Paid Override needed

2) Not in Plan but implemented/available
Display:
- Not in Plan
Actions:
- Grant Trial
- Grant Paid Override

This must work even if the feature is not linked to an Add-on Catalog item.

3) Not in Plan and linked to active add-on catalog item
Display:
- Not in Plan
- Add-on Available or Add-on

- Grant Trial
- Grant Paid Override / Grant Add-on

Paid override/add-on price should default from catalog price if linked to an active add-on.

4) Not in Plan and not linked to add-on catalog item
Display:
- Not in Plan
Actions:
- Grant Trial
- Grant Paid Override

In this case, the paid override can use feature-level/default override pricing if available, or require manual price entry.
Do not hide the action just because there is no add-on catalog link.

5) Disabled by Owner
Display:
- Disabled by Owner
Actions:
- Re-enable if applicable
- Do not show grant buttons until re-enabled, unless current design supports both

6) In Development / Not Implemented
Display:
- In Development or Not Available
Actions:
- Do not allow Trial / Paid Override until implemented

7) Trial Active
Display:
- Trial
- trial end date
Actions:
- End Trial
- Convert to Paid Override where applicable

8) Paid Override Active
Display:
- Paid Override / Overridden
- tenant-specific price if applicable
Actions:
- Edit Price
- Revoke

9) Trial Expired / Override Revoked
Display:
- Trial Expired or Revoked
Actions:
- Re-trial
- Re-grant Paid Override if feature is still available
